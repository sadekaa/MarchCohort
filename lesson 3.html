How to make Tea

This is a simple recipe on how to make Tea for beginners!
You can use any type of tea bag for this recipe whether it is PG tips or Green tea!

Ingredients

  1. 1 Teabag
  2. Boiling water
  3. Sugar (optional)

Instructions

    This will take approximately 5-7mins to make.
  • Boil kettle water
  • Put tea bag into tea cup
  • When the hot water has boiled pour water into cup with desired amount
  • Mix and allow tea bag to brew for 3mins
  • Add 1-3 teaspoons of sugar and mix (optional)
  • Squeeze teabag with spoon inside cup and then take out teabag
